President of the Student Council

During my time as Head boy in Grade 11, I had the privilege of organizing a variety of events that brought our school community together and fostered a spirit of teamwork and giving. One of the most memorable events I hosted was our Halloween celebration, with participation from over 300 students, we transformed our school into a spooky wonderland, complete with themed decorations, costume contests, and haunted house tours. Despite it being my first major event, I managed to successfully coordinate every detail, from logistics to entertainment, ensuring a seamless experience for everyone involved.

Through this endeavor, I gained invaluable experience and significantly improved my leadership, communication, and management skills. The event coincided with the final exams, adding to the challenge. Despite this, I strived to balance my responsibilities, ensuring that both the event ran smoothly and our members' academic performance was not affected. This experience has not only been rewarding but also demonstrates the power of dedication and effective teamwork.

Mái ấm Thiên Thần

Mái ấm Tân Thông

Just like the charity trip to Mái ấm Thiên Thần this charity trip to Mái ấm Tân Thông was from the profit we were able to obtain from the Halloween event. Mái ấm Tân Thông is a sanctuary for elderly men and women who are homeless, without relatives, and financially destitute. Their lives, like oil lamps flickering in the wind, are filled with uncertainty. However, thanks to Father Paul Nguyễn Văn Khi, the elders now have found a source of light and hope to guide them through their remaining years.


During our visit, we engaged the 65 residents, including 30 individuals with paralysis, in various activities like musical performances and games. The joy and gratitude on their faces were deeply moving, reinforcing the importance of our efforts. This trip was about more than just providing material support; it was about offering emotional comfort and human connection, reminding us of the power of compassion and community in bringing light to even the most uncertain lives.

Nét Bút Xanh

Recently, I had the opportunity to participate in a heartwarming initiative organized by the Nét Bút Xanh organization, aimed at providing bicycles to children who live far from their schools. These children face significant challenges in commuting daily, which often affects their attendance and overall academic performance. Understanding the impact that a simple means of transportation could have on their lives, I decided to contribute by breaking my piggy bank savings, which I had set aside for personal vacations. With the funds, I was able to help purchase bicycles and provide snacks for these children, ensuring they have the means to attend school regularly and comfortably.
